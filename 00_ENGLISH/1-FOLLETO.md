### English 

What does ADSO stand for?

It is the Spanish acronym for the 'Analysis and Development of Software' program offered by SENA. SENA is a public institution that provides free, high-quality education to Colombian citizens.

The Software Analysis and Development Technology program is part of the Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) field. It focuses on information management and software design and development. The program is currently active and is designed to train students in solving problems through algorithms using sequential, conditional, and cyclical structures.
